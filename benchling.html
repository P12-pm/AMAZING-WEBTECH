An elevated web presence for the leader in life science R&D


Traditional R&D software scatters data across silos and wipes out institutional knowledge, slowing down scientific progress. Benchling, the industry leader in cloud-based life science R&D, accelerates, measures, and forecasts essential information all on one platform.

The Silicon Valley-based technology company engaged Baunfire to build an all-new marketing website that streamlines the user journey and optimizes the path to conversion. With a lighthearted, illustrative approach, the new Benchling website tells an engaging and memorable brand story online, unlike any other in the R&D cloud services industry.



Clear and concise messaging


Our main focus for the Benchling website was to educate users efficiently and effectively, starting with the homepage. To ensure clear and consise messaging throughout, we collaborated with the communications team at Benchling to create a refined content strategy and simplify the overall structure of content on each page.

Easy-to-manage page templates

We created simple, consistent page templates, making it easier for the team at Benchling to perform updates to the website. From adding new pages to editing blocks of content, we developed a cohesive design system with an intuitive content management experience.

A refreshed brand palette


With the purpose of creating an impactful and ownable identity online, we developed a fresh and bold color palette to complement the open, light background of the website.

Inviting typography


We selected a sophisticated yet friendly type treatment for use throughout the website. Easy-to-read with a touch of personality, various weights help communicate Benchling's brand essence.

Seamlessly responsive


With an emphasis on mobile-first users, the new Benchling website is built to work seamlessly across mobile, tablet, and desktop devices.
